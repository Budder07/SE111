Breaking Bad is a critically acclaimed TV series that follows Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned ruthless drug kingpin. Diagnosed with terminal cancer, Walter partners with his former student, Jesse Pinkman, to manufacture and sell high-quality methamphetamine to secure his family's financial future. As he descends into the criminal underworld, Walter transforms from a desperate man into the feared mastermind known as "Heisenberg," facing betrayals, rival drug lords, and relentless law enforcement.
